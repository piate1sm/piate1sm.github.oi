About
My name is Sarah Piatek. I will be a graduate of Central Michigan in 2026. I am from Rockford MI and will have a BFA in graphic design with a minor in art history. I specialize in Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign and UX/UI. I do a fair amount of freelance work as well. Some of my favorite things are making stickers, watching tv, and hanging with friends and family. I also have a dog named Raylan.
